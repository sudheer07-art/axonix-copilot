About AXONIX

Your AI-Powered Career Companion for Resume Analysis, ATS Optimization, Job Matching, and Career Growth.

What is AXONIX?

AXONIX is an intelligent AI Job Search Copilot developed to simplify the hiring journey for students, freshers, and professionals.

It analyzes resumes using Artificial Intelligence, calculates ATS scores, compares resumes with job descriptions, suggests improvements, and recommends suitable live jobs.

Instead of using multiple websites for resume checking, job searching, interview preparation, and career planning, AXONIX combines everything into one modern platform.

Core Features

Resume Analysis

AI extracts skills, strengths, weaknesses and resume quality.

ATS Score

Measure how recruiter-friendly your resume is.

Live Jobs

Discover real-time job openings matching your profile.

JD Match

Compare resumes against any Job Description using AI.

AI Suggestions

Improve your resume with intelligent recommendations.

Career Guidance

Personalized insights to accelerate your career.
